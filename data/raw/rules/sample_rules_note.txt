Banking Rules and Guidelines

Customers should always follow the official instructions provided by their bank for all processes, including deposits, withdrawals, KYC, and account management.

Key Points:
- Banking rules may vary depending on the bank, branch, and account type.
- Certain processes may have limits (e.g., cash deposit limits, withdrawal limits).
- Specific documents may be mandatory based on the transaction or service.

Examples:
- KYC documents are required for account opening.
- PAN card may be needed for high-value transactions.
- Withdrawal limits may differ between ATM and branch transactions.

Note:
Always verify the latest rules with the bank's official website or customer support, as policies may change over time.
