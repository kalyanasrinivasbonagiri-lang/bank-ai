KYC Overview

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a process used by banks to verify the identity and address of customers. It is mandatory for opening and maintaining a bank account.

Purpose:
- Prevent fraud and illegal activities
- Ensure secure banking services

Common Documents Required:
- Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID)
- Address Proof (Aadhaar Card, Utility Bill, Passport)

Process:
1. Visit the bank or apply online
2. Submit KYC documents
3. Bank verifies the documents
4. KYC is approved and account becomes active

Note:
The exact document list may vary depending on bank policies and regulatory guidelines.
